Cover art and video production for the Nerds Trying To Dance EP by Pedestrian Tactics released on Mad Zoo Records

The EP was highly inspired by vocal fans who actively listened to my music while coding

A series of videos was developed to tease the release of each track on the EP using a mash of screen recordings from a Windows 95 emulator
