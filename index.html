I'm Dan

I design new and interesting experiences across a wide variety of media, utilizing a constantly expanding toolset and connections with other wonderful and talented people.

My design of the Graypants Wick was featured in Wirecutter's "28 of the Best Housewarming Gifts" and is a great example of my favorite kind of multi-faceted design effort, as I sculpted the product itself, designed the packaging and collateral, conducted user tests, and performed product photography and renderings.

I currently operate a design and electronic music brand called Pedestrian Tactics and am accepting freelance contracts.
